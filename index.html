About Publications [R]ecipes
 

Hi, I’m Sam Buckberry. I’m a postdoctoral researcher in genome bioinformatics in the Lister Lab at The University of Western Australia.

Before joining the Lister Lab in July 2015, I completed a PhD at The University of Adelaide (2012-2015). My PhD research was largely focused on developing computational methods for analysing RNA sequencing data and performing an integrative transcriptome analysis of the human fetal-maternal interface. A lot of my PhD work is publicly available, including published papers and R packages.
