About Publications CV [R]ecipes
 

Sam Buckberry is an NHMRC-ARC Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Lister Lab at The University of Western Australia. His research is focused on deciphering the role of the epigenome in neuronal gene regulation by combining cutting-edge molecular techniques and computational tools.

Before joining the Lister Lab in July 2015, Sam completed a PhD at The University of Adelaide (2012-2015). His PhD research was largely focused on developing computational methods for analysing RNA sequencing data and performing an integrative transcriptome analysis of the human fetal-maternal interface. A lot of this work is publicly available, including published papers and R packages.
